BWH - Design & Consultancy

Helping you every step of the way to the goal of an Online presence.

Whether you wish to use any of our hosted Web Software Solutions or not, you may wish to employ our services in a Web design capacity. We, can of course also help you if you also wish to take advantage of our Online services.

We know all about the difficulties and have solutions…

We have extensive knowledge of both static and dynamic WebSite design. BushWise Hosting can also help you with the planning of your project from the very beginning right through to Site roll-out fruition. Whilst learning and writing XHTML & CSS can be relatively painless if you apply enough time, energy and money at the problem, you will quickly find that just “knowing” the languages is not enough. Experience, especially applied to various Operating Systems and their plethora of available Browsers/UA's is a thing that a person cannot read/learn within books or pick up with a cursory Google search.

There is a vast knowledge base within our group spanning every popular Operating System (and other exotic variants) which can be drawn upon when any need arises surrounding a technical issue. The goal-posts, “as they say”, are a constantly changing and often infuriating thing. Trust us, we know! Many hours burning the midnight oil trying to figure out how to make what seems even the most simple things work consistently can be enough to try even the patience of a Saint.

A little bit of background about our team…

Our design team have worked closely with, been employed by and learnt a lot from some of the worlds leading designers and typographers including (just to name drop a little) Neville Brody and Eric Spiekerman. They work in conjunction with our technical, editorial and if required, legal teams on every project to provide support wherever it is needed. So if you wish to have a Site designed for you personally or a full blown Corporate venture we can help at every step along the way to make your dream come alive on the World Wide Web.
