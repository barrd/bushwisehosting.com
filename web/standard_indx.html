BWH - Standard WebSite Hosting

Wish to have a personal or Business WebSite hosted?

We laughingly call this “The Standard Hosting” Solution. Amusing because it is far from “basic” or any other thoughts that spring to mind regarding a “standard” service. All our hosted Sites come with a broad range of available modules allowing any client to manage their Site as they see fit. Your Site can grow, if, and when, you decide to add more content or different areas. You may however only want a limited Solution to meet your needs. This could include, only as a very rough guideline, the following:

Personal Hosted WebSite Example:
  • A HomePage
    (A starting 'jump-in' point for any site)
  • An “About Me” Page
    (Basics about who you are)
  • Contact Details Page
    (Where you are and how to contact you)
  • Hobbies & Interests Pages
    (Further information 'about you')
  • Photo Galleries
    (Share your pictures with the world)
  • Private/Shared - Blogs
    (Have your say with a weblog)
Business Hosted WebSite Example:
  • A HomePage
    (A starting 'jump-in' point for any site)
  • Overview of Products/Services
    (What you have to offer)
  • Contact Details Page
    (Where you are and how to contact you)
  • Detailed Products/Services Pages
    (More info about your business & products)
  • Products/Services Galleries
    (Showcase your products and/or services)
  • Company Updates/News Page
    (Up to date news about your business)

You get the idea… We mention this as we are aware not everyone wishes to have or maintain an all “bells and whistles” concern. Our Standard Hosting Solution will cater for a personal Site with only a handful of pages to be easily created, updated and managed, or also allow a Company or other business venture a presence on the Internet that need not be huge or overly dynamic.

All sites, regardless of nature are Database driven which enables us to provide you with the Content Management System - CMS, which controls your Site. This is a very powerful tool and explains, in part, why our so called “Standard Hosting” package is not so standard…
