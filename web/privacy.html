Introduction

BushWise Hosting Ltd is committed to safeguarding your privacy Online. This Privacy Policy will allow you to understand how your personal information is used on the BushWise Hosting Ltd and associates Sites. This policy will change from time to time (see Section 13 of this Privacy Policy), so please check back to this page periodically.

1.Sites: bushwise.net / bushwisehosting.com

1.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd is an IT Hosting Company based in the United Kingdom but as a “Virtual Company” has ties, partners, and associates in a global capacity.

2. What this Privacy Policy covers.

2.1

This Privacy Policy covers the treatment of personally identifiable information which is collected when you visit BushWise Hosting Ltd. It does not apply to other services supplied by BushWise Hosting Ltd's partners such as eCommerce or any other affiliated service (see Section 7).

3. What personal information is collected by BushWise Hosting Ltd and when is it collected?

3.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd collects information in a number of ways from users at different parts of the Site. In particular, personal information is collected when users register for a Site as a whole or complete a transaction. Registration is necessary in order to complete a transaction. BushWise Hosting Ltd will also ask users for personal information when users enter competitions or apply for special offers, take part in research via surveys, or submit eMails. Information requested will include contact details including name and eMail address. If you contact BushWise Hosting Ltd or any of its associates we may keep a record of that correspondence.

4. What are Cookies and log files and how does BushWise Hosting Ltd use them?

4.1 Cookies:

A Cookie is a message given by a Web Browser (which displays your chosen Web pages on your computer) to a Web Server (which delivers the Web pages to your computer). The message is stored by your Browser and will be used each time you access the Site which the Cookie relates to.

4.2 Log Files:

BushWise Hosting Ltd uses IP (Internet Protocol) addresses to analyse trends, administer the Site, track users movements, and to gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. All users to the Internet have an IP address, which enables Networks connected to the Internet to transmit the data and messages submitted by users from one host to another and to ensure messages are understood. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information and therefore the information BushWise Hosting Ltd produces using IP addresses does not enable individual users to be identified.

5. How does BushWise Hosting Ltd use users personal information?

5.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd may process the information that we collect from you and from your use of the Sites for the following purposes:

  1. Provision of our services.
  2. Administration of member and subscription records.
  3. To notify you about changes to our WebSite and services.
  4. Record-keeping for up to six years following termination of our services to you.
  5. Administration of our competitions, promotions and special offers.
  6. Technical maintenance.
  7. To inform you about services which we, our partners, clients and associated companies offer.
  8. To work with sponsors and promoters who wish to provide targeted messages to our users.
  9. To work with parties and sponsors on special offers, promotions and competitions.
  10. Operation and enforcement of our terms and conditions and acceptable use policies.
  11. To transfer our business to a purchaser.
  12. Compliance with laws and applicable regulations and orders of courts and other competent tribunals.
  13. Co-operation with the Police or any other Government body.
  14. Investigation of claims that your use of our services infringes any right (including our intellectual property rights) or breaches any law, rule, regulation or order and disclosure of your contact details and usage details to the legal advisers of any person claiming that your use of our services infringes any right or breaches any law, rule, regulation or order.

In addition, BushWise Hosting Ltd carries out research on user demographics, interests, and behaviour based on the information provided to us directly (for example during eCommerce transactions) or from log files. The aim of this is to better understand and serve users. This research is compiled and analysed on an aggregated basis and does not enable identification of individual users.

6. Does anyone other than BushWise Hosting Ltd collect information from the BushWise Hosting Ltd Site?

6.1

Except as stated in this Privacy Policy or unless you are informed at the time of collection of information, only BushWise Hosting Ltd collect personal information from this Site.

7. Do BushWise Hosting Ltd or any of it's affiliates share user information?

7.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone. Except as described in this Privacy Policy, BushWise Hosting Ltd will not disclose or share your personally identifiable information. The following describes the ways in which user information may be shared.

7.2 Partners, business partners & sponsors:

BushWise Hosting Ltd may disclose aggregate user information to carefully selected organisations, business partners or sponsors or potential advertisers. For example aggregated data collected in BushWise Hosting Ltd may be shared with a partner for the purposes of assessing the promotion. It is not possible to identify individual users from such aggregated data.

Occasionally BushWise Hosting Ltd will work with sponsors and partners who wish to provide targeted messages to our users. We will tell you about this when you register or at the point when we collect your information. You will be able to opt out of receiving such messages (see Section 8). BushWise Hosting Ltd will collate the information and deliver the eMail on the partner's behalf.

7.3 eMail a friend:

If you elect to use our referral service for informing a friend about our Site, BushWise Hosting Ltd will ask you for your friend's name and eMail address. BushWise Hosting Ltd will automatically send your friend a one-time eMail inviting them to visit the Site. BushWise Hosting Ltd will store your friend's name and eMail address for the sole purpose of sending this one-time eMail and for no other reason or use. You should only give us your friend's eMail address if your friend is happy for you to do so.

7.4 External Links:

BushWise Hosting Ltd contains links to other Sites. Please be aware that BushWise Hosting Ltd is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other Sites. BushWise Hosting Ltd encourages users to be aware when they leave the Site and to read the privacy statements of each and every WebSite that collects personally identifiable information.

8. What are your choices regarding collection, use, and distribution of your personally identifiable information?

8.1

We will always either ask for your consent or give you the option to opt in to receiving offers or information from BushWise Hosting Ltd or its partners, either at registration or when we collect your information. In addition, at any time you can contact us to request not to receive such offers and mailings and to have your information removed (see Section 9).

9. What is BushWise Hosting Ltd's policy on allowing users to update, correct, or delete their personally identifiable information?

9.1

Please Note:
All information (excluding Credit Card details - they are never at any time stored anywhere) will be permanently stored with no edits permitted regarding any and all commercial sales. This is required by law.

9.2

If you wish to update or amend any other information held by BushWise Hosting Ltd (for example to change contact details or to alter preferences with regard to receiving mailings), please contact us. To update or amend information or preferences held by a third party, for example sponsors of a competition, you will need to contact that third party.

BushWise Hosting Ltd registrations can be deleted or deactivated. Users should contact us for further instructions about deleting or deactivating a BushWise Hosting Ltd registration.

10. What other security precautions should users take?

10.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd recommend that users do not divulge their BushWise Hosting Ltd registration password to anyone. BushWise Hosting Ltd will never ask users for passwords in an unsolicited phone call or in an unsolicited eMail. Users should also remember to log off from their BushWise Hosting Ltd account and close their Browser window when they have finished work. This is to ensure that others cannot access your personal information and correspondence if you share a computer with someone else or are using a computer in a public place such as a library or Internet café.

Ultimately, you are solely responsible for keeping your passwords and/or account information secret. You should be careful and responsible whenever you are Online.

11. Transfer of personally identifiable data overseas.

11.1

Simple. We do not, have not and will not send users personally identifiable data overseas as we cannot 100% guarantee that other countries Data Protection laws will cover your privacy.

12. Children.

12.1

BushWise Hosting Ltd advises that children (under 12) do not register for its services or participate in its competitions and promotions.

13. Notification of Changes.

13.1

If BushWise Hosting Ltd decides to change its Privacy Policy, it will post changes on this Privacy Policy page so that users are always aware of what information is collected, how it is used and the way in which information may be disclosed. If BushWise Hosting Ltd makes substantial changes to the way personal information is used, users will be notified by eMail.
