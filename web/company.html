BWH - Company Details

Everything you wanted to know about us but were afraid to ask…

This page aims to provide an overview of everything about BushWise Hosting, from contact details to our philosophy and client relations policies. If you wish to send us a mail, please go to the contact page. Our postal address and other contact methods may be found here.

A short introduction to the Company:

The staff is made up of full time employees and contracted consultants (based around the globe). By use of IRC, VoIP, VideoConferencing and a host of other Internet based communication applications the team work closely together to see any bespoke projects carefully completed within an arranged time scale and cost. Day to day services surrounding Hosting of Sites and their various functions are handled and supported by professionals on a 24/7 basis.
