BWH - Content Management System

The basics surrounding Content Management Systems:

What is a CMS? A BWH Content Management System - CMS is a Software programme used to manage and organise the content of a BushWise hosted WebSite. A CMS allows the Content Managers and/or authors of the WebSite, who may not know HyperText Markup Language - HTML the Markup language used for the Web, to create, modify, remove and organise the information and graphics on their WebSite. Among the many virtues of a CMS application for a WebSite is the fact that it allows for multiple editors to add, amend and publish WebSite content in real time via the Internet, thereby saving the time and expense of sending updates to designers. This is especially true when you notice something and want to edit it whilst fresh in your mind. Pieces of paper, “stickies” and other note taking methods often get lost, misplaced or forgotten. Imagine the power of having your Site at your finger tips anytime, and anywhere you have access to the Internet…

Our approach to Content Management:

What can we offer that is different? We can enable you and if applicable, members of your staff to update WebSites by means of a user friendly Management Interface, cutting out the need to know anything about complicated Markup or programming languages1. There are a thousand other Sites offering what seems to be the same solution package, what makes us different? We have a large Suite of additional modules making your Site as extensible as you want/need it to be. Many others will allow you to pad out predefined templates which you have no access to, we however allow you to use, create, design and edit any templates you desire. This has many advantages, allowing you to create a consistent Site with standarised Header, Footer and other layout elements that can be shared amongst many pages. Each element (template) can be edited with conditionals to help function as a fully dynamic Site which will enhance any potential clients visit to your hosted WebSite. The CMS Software can be used for every aspect of your Site, whether static, dynamic, basic information rendering or commercial.

1: Advanced features in the Software will require some learning.
